Sousuke Kouta (十日草輔)

He was born in 1975 in Japan, there is no English Wikipedia of the author.

According to Japanese Wikipedia, his favourite works are Dragon Ball and Ultimate Muscle.

He wanted to become a mangaka (Japanese comic author) since when he was 20 years old. One Piece was one of the works that heavily inspired him.

There is no official picture of the author, so the one in Curiosity is a random person found on Google Pictures.

In the interview with ANN, the author said:

"My parents don't know anything about it. They still think I am working as a salaryman. They are old-fashioned and conservative types who have no understanding of manga as a career. I am afraid that they will worry about me if they find out that I make a living by drawing manga, so I keep it secret from them."
