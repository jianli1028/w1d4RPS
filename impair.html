Disabilities

The protagonist Bojji is a impaired kid, he is in fact deaf, but this does not stop him from being strong and kind to others, this is why he is loved by everyone around him.

Some people are born with disabilities, but others become impaired due to many reasons, many of these are invisible to our eyes.

Bojji, even as the Prince of his kingdom, he was looked down by his people because of his deafness. His people didn't know he could read lips, and blatantly trash-talked him while he was passing by the streets, this did hurt him a lot, but he never and ever hated them.

In Ranking of Kings, it shows how much a deaf person can appreciate us learning the sign language, seeing others putting so much effor for them makes them feel loved and appreciated.
